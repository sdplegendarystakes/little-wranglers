Meet Your Counselor

Hi, I'm Maggie!

I've grown up around horses my whole life right here at SDP Buffalo Ranch in Fort Worth. Horses have taught me patience, confidence, and responsibility — and I can't wait to share that with your kids.

The Little Wrangler Ranch Camp is my very own summer program where I give kids a real, hands-on ranch experience. Every camper gets my undivided attention with true one-on-one instruction — no crowds, no waiting, just you, me, and the horses.

Whether your child has never been near a horse or just wants to build their skills, I'll meet them exactly where they are and make sure they leave feeling proud, confident, and excited about ranch life.

What to Expect

A Day at the Ranch

Every day is a new adventure. Here's a taste of what your little wrangler will experience.

🐴

Riding Lessons

One-on-one instruction covering mounting, steering, stopping, and building confidence in the saddle — at your child's pace.

🖌️

Grooming & Care

Brushing, mane care, and learning how to cool down and bathe horses — hands-on care that builds a real bond.

🎒

Tack & Equipment

Learning saddle and bridle names, helping tack a horse up, and understanding tack safety and organization.

🌾

Ranch Chores

Cleaning stalls, filling water buckets, feeding basics — real ranch responsibility that builds character.

🌿

Trail Rides

Supervised beginner trail rides that build outdoor riding confidence and a love for the open range.

🏅

Friday Showcase

Parents are invited to watch a special end-of-week showcase where campers show off everything they've learned.

Your Child's Safety Is Everything

Safe, Supervised & Fun

  • True one-on-one instruction — your child is never alone with a horse
  • Helmets are strongly encouraged for all riding activities
  • Beginner-friendly, gentle horses selected for children
  • Safety-first environment with ranch rules communicated on day one
  • Regular water breaks and included snack break each session
  • Parent communication throughout the week — you're always in the loop
  • Allergy-aware — please share any food allergies when booking
  • Please bring your child's water bottle each day
📋

Liability Waiver Required

All campers must have a completed and signed SDP Buffalo Ranch liability waiver on file before their first day of camp. This is required by Texas law for farm animal activities.

Download the waiver below, complete it at home, and bring it signed on the first day.

⬇ Download Waiver (PDF)

Under Texas law (Chapter 87, Civil Practice and Remedies Code), a farm animal professional is not liable for injuries resulting from the inherent risks of farm animal activities.

Policies & Info

Deposit & Payment

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to reserve your spot. The remaining balance is due before your child's first day of camp.

Cancellation Policy

Deposits are non-refundable due to limited availability and one-on-one scheduling. Cancellations made at least 30 days before camp may be eligible for a refund — contact Maggie to discuss.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Summer plans change! Flexible scheduling options are available to accommodate travel and family schedules. Just reach out and we'll make it work.

🍎

Snacks & Allergies

Snacks are included each session. Please notify Maggie of any food allergies when booking. Always bring your child's own water bottle.
